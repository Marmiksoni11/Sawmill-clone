THE SMOKO ROOM

Having moved from the original site in Leigh,
the Sawmill Brewery has been turning out beer
from premises just out of Matakana since
February 2016.The end of July 2016 we opened
the doors to visitors and locals to experience
the kind of hospitality Sawmill is about -
straight forward and generous.

MENU

Our menu celebrates the farmers and growers we work with.
Ingredients are ethically sourced, seasonal and availability is often
weather dependent. Our cheeses, olive oil, kaimoana, beef, bread,
olives, fruit and vegetables are largely sourced from the
Mahurangi region.

Our food is created to share amongst friends and family. Each dish
is served as ready.

RESERVATIONS

DAYTIME

We are a walk-in only venue between 12pm - 5.30pm. Just come on in at
any time and we will accommodate you on your arrival.

If you're a group of 20 or more people, please see info on group bookings
below.

DINNER

Reservations indoors for the Smoko Room can be made for dinner from
5.30pm onwards for any number of guests. To make a reservation email
smokoroom@sawmillbrewery.co.nz or call 09 422 6555 (ext. 1).

We don't take reservations for our outdoor area so please come early to
secure a table if you'd like to dine outside.

GROUP BOOKINGS

Reservations of 20 or more adults are required to order from one of our
'trust us' set menus. Please get in touch with us to find out more and to
reserve a table: smokoroom@sawmillbrewery.co.nz.

Reservations for groups of 20 or more adults can be made any time
throughout the day.

WEDDINGS

We're humbled by the thought of you wanting us to host your wedding at
the Smoko Room. Although we don't cater to wedding ceremonies and
receptions, we are the perfect spot to catch up with friends and family the
day before or after. Please email smokoroom@sawmillbrewery.co.nz to
find out how we can help make this an exceptional occasion.
